The giant panda has been reclassified from "endangered" to "vulnerable", indicating a positive trend in conservation efforts.
Giant Panda Conservation
Conservation efforts for the giant panda include habitat preservation, anti-poaching measures, and research on panda behavior and health. Successful breeding programs in captivity are also crucial for increasing their population.
